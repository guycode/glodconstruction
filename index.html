Services

Ways we can help you in start to rebuild your home and life.

Kitchens

The kitchen is a space where time is spent eating, cooking, talking and reading. With most of the time being spent there, the space should be comfortable and look great. Whether its countertops, cabinets, painting, or flooring, our team will make sure that your ideal kitchen is created so that you and your family can enjoy it for years to come.

Basements

Whether you’re looking to create a beautiful basement, finish the space in order to conserve energy, or even if you’re unsure of what to have, the group at Glod Construction will work with you on the design and provide you with input and options that you can choose from.

Bathrooms

Glod Construction can create and build a clean, simple and elegant bathroom for your home. Our team is trained to employ skilled craftsmanship for your bathroom renovation project.

Flooring

Flooring makes all the difference when finishing a room. From hardwood flooring to ceramic, we pride ourselves on keeping promises and keeping our valued customers happy.

Painting

From outside to inside, we can handle all your business or home painting and stainging needs. Guarenteed to give your space or building a welcomed facelift every time.

Custom Millwork

Our team of experienced carpenters actively engages home owners from the design process and carries that communication through to the building and installation phases to create beautiful millwork.
